Peter Keler, a versatile German artist, was one of the representatives of famous Bauhaus art movement in Germany, which was typical for its functionality and consistency with mass production. Keler was a painter, architect and graphic artist, whose pieces are popular and sought for even nowadays, because of their attractive simplicity. Keler's interior design objects, like chairs and cradles, are so successful that they are still being sold by the German company called Tecta.
